# Career Goals

My long-term goal is to continuously grow as a Software Engineer and AI Engineer by working on challenging real-world problems and building products that create meaningful impact.

I believe that learning never stops. One of my biggest motivations is constantly improving my technical skills, exploring new technologies, and becoming a better engineer every year. I enjoy challenging myself with complex projects because they help me learn faster and expand my knowledge.

In the coming years, I want to build a strong career in backend software engineering, artificial intelligence, and distributed systems. I am particularly interested in designing scalable backend architectures, AI-powered applications, automation platforms, and modern cloud-based systems.

I also aspire to gain international work experience by joining a global technology company where I can collaborate with talented engineers, work on products used by millions of people, and continue learning from experienced professionals.

One of my personal goals is to relocate and work abroad, especially in an environment that values innovation, engineering excellence, and continuous learning. I believe that working in an international team will help me grow both technically and professionally.

In the long term, I hope to build products and companies of my own. I enjoy transforming ideas into practical software solutions, and I would like to create AI-powered products that solve real business problems and improve people's daily work.

Above all, I want to build a career based on continuous learning, curiosity, discipline, and delivering high-quality software that makes a real difference.
